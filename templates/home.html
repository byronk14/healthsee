Welcome to healthsee!

Ever wondered how your access to to healthcare compares to others in the US? This project is designed to give you access to information about your healthcare based on your location.

Information on this topic is hard to digest, so we made a simple report which shows you how access to healthcare in your area compares to other regions in the US. HealthSee uses publically available data sets leveraging the power of python via pandas, flask, and boken to give you access to healthcare information in a simple dashboard view.
